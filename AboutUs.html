ABOUT US

Para sa Street is a local Filipino streetwear brand, established on March 13, 2023, by EMANUEL B. DE GUIA. We are dedicated to crafting high-quality and stylish apparel for the urban fashion scene.

We aim to fuse contemporary fashion trends with the vibrant essence of Filipino urban culture. Our apparel is designed to empower self-expression, helping you discover and showcase your unique style every day. Experience comfort and flair, perfectly suited for the dynamic Philippine streets.
